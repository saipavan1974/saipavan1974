Key Features

These feature descriptions are written in a way that supports Innovator Founder endorsement: innovation + viability + scalability.

Asset Inventory & Lifecycle

Track devices and equipment, ownership, warranties, lifecycle stages, and maintenance history with an audit trail.

Health Scoring Engine

Converts measurable signals into a health score (rule-based initially). Roadmap includes anomaly detection and predictive modelling.

Risk Alerts

Early warning notifications to prevent downtime. Prioritise what matters first with a risk-based queue.

Preventative Scheduling

Automates routine tasks (patching, backups, health checks). Assigns tasks and records outcomes for continuous improvement.

Reports for SMEs

Clear metrics: downtime avoided, recurring incidents, high-risk assets, and maintenance ROI—simple for SME owners.

Integrations (Roadmap)

Email/Slack alerts, ticketing connectors, and exports. Later: API-first integrations for partners and MSPs.
