Scalability Plan

BPK is built as a platform business (SaaS) so it can scale beyond local services: remote onboarding, partner delivery, and recurring subscription revenue.

Target Markets

  • UK SMEs: retail, logistics, clinics, offices, small manufacturing
  • Managed Service Providers (MSPs) needing simple asset intelligence
  • Multi-site SMEs needing standardised maintenance workflows

Growth Model

  • Pilot (10–100 assets) → expand organisation-wide
  • Remote onboarding + templates reduce delivery cost
  • Partner model: MSPs onboard clients using BPK

Job Creation

  • Year 1: Founder-led + part-time support/contract dev
  • Year 2: Hire 1 platform engineer + 1 customer success
  • Year 3: Hire data/ops analyst + sales/partnership role
