How Support Works

A simple workflow clients understand: intake, triage, action, prevention, and clear closure notes.

1

Intake

Report issues via WhatsApp, email, or form. Include device/user/site and any screenshots if possible.

2

Triage

We confirm severity (Critical/High/Normal) and agree the next action (remote guidance or onsite).

3

Action

We restore service quickly and document what was changed (fixes, updates, parts, recommendations).

4

Prevention

If the issue is recurring, we add preventative tasks into your schedule to stop repeat downtime.

Support FAQ

Common questions from SME clients.

Do you provide onsite support? Yes

Yes. Onsite support is available for onboarding, key repairs, and network/device checks. Platform-led preventative maintenance remains the primary model.

What do clients receive after resolution? Summary

You receive a clear summary: what caused the issue, what changed, and recommendations to prevent recurrence. Repeat issues are added into the preventative schedule.

Can you help choose the right SSD/RAM before purchase? Compatibility

Yes. Send your laptop model (or a photo of the label) and we’ll confirm compatibility and recommended specs. If you buy via the Shop page, checkout happens on Amazon.

Is break-fix included in the platform subscription? Optional

Break-fix is optional. The platform subscription focuses on tracking, health scoring, risk alerts, scheduling, and reporting. Repairs/upgrades can be added as required.

Do you support backups and recovery? Add-on

Yes. We validate backup strategy, run restore tests, and document evidence for business continuity. Recovery actions depend on device condition and client-approved storage.
