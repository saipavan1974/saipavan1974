Support FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you do home visits? Yes

Yes — home visits are available within around 10 miles of Preston (subject to time/availability). Home visit pricing is on the pricing page.

How long will my repair take? Depends

It depends on the problem and parts availability. Many software fixes can be same day. Upgrades (RAM/SSD) are usually quick once confirmed compatible.

Can you install parts I buy from your shop? Yes

Yes. After purchasing from the shop page, you can book installation from the pricing page or message us for the quickest option.

Do you help choose the correct RAM/SSD? Yes

Yes — send your laptop model (or a photo of the bottom label) and we’ll confirm the correct RAM/SSD type and recommended size.

Do you recover deleted files? Sometimes

Sometimes — success depends on the drive condition and whether new data has overwritten the old files. We’ll do an assessment first and explain your options.
