Support Services

A clear and repeatable support model that clients trust: onboarding first, then ongoing health scoring, risk alerts, preventative schedules, and measurable outcomes.

Onboarding & Asset Discovery

Get set up quickly with consistent data, correct owners, and baseline health scoring.

  • Asset inventory creation (devices, users, sites)
  • Baseline health checks (storage, battery, performance, errors)
  • Tagging: by location, department, owner and lifecycle stage
  • Initial risk scoring + “top issues” summary
  • Implementation checklist and quick-start guides

Security Hardening

Practical SME security improvements without slowing down your team.

  • Patch/update guidance and maintenance windows
  • Endpoint protection checks + malware clean-up support
  • Account hygiene: admin rights, password policy guidance
  • Phishing awareness and secure usage guidance
  • Basic security posture recommendations (SME-friendly)

Break-Fix (Optional)

When something fails, we restore service quickly—but the platform remains the primary model.

  • Fault diagnosis and recovery actions
  • Performance optimisation and maintenance tasks
  • OS install support (where required)
  • Hardware upgrades (RAM/SSD) and replacement guidance
  • Clear documentation of work done + recommendations

Backup & Recovery

Reduce risk with backup checks and restore testing aligned to business continuity.

SME IT Optimisation

Improve reliability and performance across devices and network basics.

  • Wi-Fi / network stability checks (SME-level)

  • Standardisation suggestions (builds, tools, policies)
  • Recommendations tied to measurable outcomes

Reporting & Outcomes

How Support Works

A simple, professional workflow that clients understand. Clear steps, response times, and outcomes.

1
Intake

Raise a request via WhatsApp, email, or our form with key details.

2
Triage

We confirm priority (Critical/High/Normal) and the next best action.

3
Action

Remote guidance first; onsite support when it’s genuinely required.

4
Prevention

Root cause is logged and preventative actions are scheduled.

5
Close & Report

Short summary shared: what happened, what changed, and what’s next.

Response SLAs (Typical)

These are baseline targets; exact SLAs can be set by plan and customer agreement.

Targets (Business Hours)

  • Critical: response within 2–4 hours
  • High: response within 8–24 hours
  • Normal: response within 1–2 business days
  • Planned changes: scheduled maintenance windows

If your team operates outside standard hours, we can discuss extended coverage.

What we track

  • Time to first response
  • Time to resolution (where measurable)
  • Recurring issues and root cause categories
  • Preventative tasks completed vs planned
  • Asset risk trend over time

This helps you see the ROI: fewer incidents, lower downtime, better reliability.

Raise a Support Request

  • Asset details: device name/model/serial (if available)

Support FAQ

Answers to common client questions. If you want, we can tailor this FAQ to your exact support plans.

Do you provide onsite support? Yes

Yes. Onsite support is available, especially for onboarding, key repairs, and network/device checks. Primary focus remains platform-led preventative maintenance.

What do clients receive after an issue is resolved? Summary

A short resolution summary: what caused the issue, what changed, and recommendations to prevent recurrence. For recurring issues, we add preventative actions into the schedule.

Can you help choose the right SSD/RAM before purchase? Compatibility

Yes. Send your laptop model (or a photo of the label) and we’ll confirm compatibility and a recommended spec. If you buy via the Shop page, checkout is on Amazon.

Is break-fix included in the platform subscription? Optional

Break-fix is optional. The platform subscription focuses on tracking, health scoring, risk alerts, scheduling and reporting. Repairs/upgrades can be added as required.

Do you support data backups and recovery? Add-on

Yes. We help you validate backup strategy, perform restore tests, and document evidence for business continuity. Recovery actions depend on device condition and client-approved storage.

What are your typical support hours? Hours

Support is currently aligned to weekend business hours (Sat–Sun). If you need weekday or extended coverage, we can discuss a plan based on your asset count and service requirements.
