Privacy Policy

At BPK Computer Services, we value your privacy and are committed to complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR). Any personal information you provide (such as name, phone number, or email) will be used solely to respond to your inquiries or process service requests.

We do not share your data with third parties, and we ensure it is stored securely. If you contact us via forms or email, your information will only be used for the intended communication and will not be retained longer than necessary.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To do so, please contact us at bpk@bpkcomputer.com.
